More than 10 years in the digital world

My career in design UI/UX and front-end development.

I studied Visual Design at the Minuto de Dios University in Medellin, where I discovered my passion for the digital world. From an early age, I loved everything related to the digital realm.

In my early days, the roles of UI, UX designer, and programmer weren't as well-defined, which led me to learn programming out of market necessity. This experience provided me with a holistic view of digital creation.

Thanks to this, I've had the opportunity to work with clients from around the world, enriching my perspective and expanding my professional horizon.
